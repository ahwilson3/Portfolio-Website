About Me

Adam is currently enrolled in the Web Design and UX Design programs at Wake Tech. He was born and raised in Raleigh, North Carolina, and currently works full-time as a Web Designer for an online marketing company called Autoshop Solutions. They specialize in helping automotive repair shops get found on Google through SEO, creating custom websites, posting social media, and running ads on Google.

It is his goal to complete both associate degrees and apply for a Web Design position within his company. Adam loves designing websites and is looking forward to getting experience and working his way to becoming an excellent web designer.
